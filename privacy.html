Listee is a "local-only" tool. We do not have a database. We do not see your Vinted password, your messages, or your items. All automation happens directly on your computer browser.

1. Introduction

The "Listee" Chrome Extension ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data in compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and UK Data Protection Act.

2. Data We Collect (and Don't Collect)

2.1 Personal Data

We do not transmit, store, or sell your personal data. We do not have a backend server. We cannot see:

  • Your login credentials (passwords).
  • Your payment information.
  • Your personal messages.

2.2 Usage Data (Website Content)

The extension temporarily accesses specific content on the web pages you visit (specifically, Vinted item pages) solely for the purpose of executing the "Copy" and "Paste" automation commands. This data (titles, descriptions, photos) is stored temporarily in your browser's local memory (`chrome.storage.local`) and is overwritten or deleted immediately after the task is complete.

3. Permissions Explained

We require specific browser permissions to function. Here is why:

  • activeTab / Scripting: Required to read the text on your "Old Item" page and paste it into the "New Item" form.
  • Storage: Required to hold that text in your computer's memory for the few seconds between the "Copy" and "Paste" actions.

4. Third-Party Services

The extension is hosted on the Google Chrome Web Store. Google may collect aggregated, anonymous technical data (e.g., number of installs, crash reports) subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

5. Your Rights (GDPR)

Under the GDPR, you have rights regarding your data. However, since Listee does not collect or store your personal data on our servers, we hold no data to provide, correct, or delete.

If you wish to clear all data associated with the extension, simply uninstall the extension from your browser. This immediately removes all locally stored temporary data.
